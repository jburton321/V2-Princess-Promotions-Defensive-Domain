Understanding the Program

What Is Princess Promotions, Exactly?

This is the first thing most people get wrong, and it's the source of a lot of the frustration we found in complaints.

Princess Promotions is not a standalone travel company. It's an onboard sales program connected to Princess Cruises, operated through a third-party company called Open Network Exchange (ONE Inc.).

You'll encounter it during a sales presentation while already on a Princess Cruise ship, or through a follow-up phone call after you've booked.

What they sell are Future Cruise Packages — bundles including Future Cruise Credits (FCCs worth $1 each), hotel stay certificates, hotel credits ($600–$4,000), onboard credits, and sometimes Elite status.

Entity Confusion

Is Princess Promotions the Same as Princess Cruises?

No — and this distinction matters more than most realize.

Princess Promotions operates on Princess ships using the Princess brand, but it's run by a separate company (ONE Inc.). Think of it like a third-party vendor inside a department store.

When something goes wrong, each entity may point you to the other. Multiple BBB complaints describe this runaround.

"Multiple BBB complaints describe customers being bounced between the two entities with no one taking full responsibility."— From our analysis of 2025–2026 BBB complaint data

The Question Everyone Asks

Is It a Scam? No.
Is It Complicated? Yes.

The Future Cruise Credits are real and work. But there are documented, recurring patterns of misleading sales practices — particularly around hotel credits, total cost, and post-sale accessibility.

The gap between what customers were told and what the fine print says is wide enough that multiple people have used the word "scam" — even though the underlying product is legitimate.

Our take: Not a scam, but the sales process has serious transparency issues. Read the findings below before you commit.

How It Works

FCCs Auto-Apply When You Book

When you book a Princess Cruise, your credits apply automatically to the fare. You can also call to have them applied manually to an existing reservation. Credits can be split across multiple bookings.

Know the Limits

What FCCs Don't Cover

Onboard expenses, shore excursions, Princess Plus and Premier packages, and restricted-rate EZair airfare are all excluded. FCCs do work for Flexible Air add-ons.

Protect Yourself

Screenshot Everything

Capture your FCC balance before and after every transaction. Request written confirmation of how credits were applied. This is your best protection against the accounting issues described above.

Hotel Redemption

How to Use Princess Promotions Hotel Certificates and Credits

Unlike FCCs, these are managed by Princess Promotions directly. Call 1-800-PRINCESS, option 5.

Credits may apply at 50% face value. This is the most complained-about issue. $600 in credits may mean only $300 usable. Get written confirmation before purchasing.

Compare prices first. Hotel rates on the platform are often higher than Booking.com, Hotels.com, and Expedia.

Check expiration dates immediately. Hotel stay certificates expire. Confirm dates at purchase and set calendar reminders. Certificates are non-transferable.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions
About Princess Promotions

Is Princess Promotions a Scam?

Based on our review, no. FCCs are real and work. However, there are documented issues with hotel credits, additional charges, and post-sale support. The product is legitimate, but the sales process has transparency issues.

Is Princess Promotions the Same as Princess Cruises?

No. Operated by Open Network Exchange (ONE Inc.), a third-party. FCCs go through Princess Cruises; hotel certificates through Princess Promotions.

How Do Hotel Credits Actually Work?

May apply at 50% of stated value — $600 = $300 usable. Platform prices often higher than Booking.com and Expedia. Always compare and get written confirmation.

What if I Can't Reach My Planning Advisor?

Call the main line and request supervisor escalation. Document every call. If within the cancellation window, send written cancellation via email immediately.

Can I Cancel My Package?

7-day cancellation window from purchase. After that, significantly harder. Send written cancellation immediately — don't rely on phone calls alone.

Why Was I Charged More Than Quoted?

Multiple BBB complaints describe this. Total cost may include installments not explained at sale. Get a written breakdown of everything before purchasing.

Are Cruise Credits Cheaper Than Booking Direct?

FCCs provide real savings, especially for frequent cruisers. Hotel components often don't match paper value. Evaluate FCC value separately from hotel value.

Our Verdict

The Final Word on
Princess Promotions

The Future Cruise Credits work. They apply to bookings as described. For frequent cruisers, the FCC math often makes the package worth it.

The hotel credits are where value breaks down. 50% redemption, inflated pricing, certificate expiration — all erode what looks generous on paper.

The sales process needs improvement. Gap between verbal promises and fine print. Unreliable post-sale support. Cancellation window too short for purchases this large.

If cruise credits alone justify the price — reasonable deal.
If you need hotel credits to make the math work — proceed with caution.
